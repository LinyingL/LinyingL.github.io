This research investigated the impact of YouTube on public discourse regarding income inequality.

### Key Methods:
- **BERTopic**: Identified diverse narratives across video content.
- **Fine-tuned BERT**: Classified political stances in a massive dataset of 340,000+ comments.
- **Krackhardt E-I Index**: Measured network interaction homogeneity to understand echo chambers.
